# Acceptable Use Policy — TEMPLATE

> Draft. Attorney review required.

**Effective Date:** `[to be filled]`

By using the VouchRail Service or Software (the "Service"), Customer agrees
to refrain from the following activities. Violation may result in
suspension or termination per the Terms of Service.

## Prohibited uses

Customer shall not:

1. Use the Service to record, attest, or pseudonymize personal data
   collected unlawfully.
2. Use the Service to manufacture, alter, or backdate audit log entries
   that do not faithfully represent the operation of Customer's AI system.
3. Attempt to bypass, disable, or modify the Service's tamper-evidence
   mechanisms (hash chain, signing, WORM enforcement).
4. Use the Service to process Personal Data without a lawful basis under
   applicable data protection law.
5. Use the Service to support an AI system whose operation is unlawful in
   the jurisdictions where Customer or its data subjects are located
   (e.g., systems prohibited under EU AI Act Article 5).
6. Use the Service to compete with VouchRail's hosted offerings (e.g.,
   wrap the Service for resale without a written reseller agreement).
7. Reverse-engineer, decompile, or scrape the Service except as expressly
   permitted by applicable law or the Apache 2.0 license terms applicable
   to the open-source Software.
8. Use the Service to send unsolicited communications, distribute malware,
   conduct denial-of-service attacks, or otherwise abuse third parties or
   VouchRail's infrastructure.

## Reporting abuse

Report abuse to `abuse@vouchrail.com`.

## Enforcement

VouchRail may suspend the Service to investigate suspected violations.
Where possible, we will provide notice before suspension; in case of
imminent harm, suspension may occur without prior notice.
